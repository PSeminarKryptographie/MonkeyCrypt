Can you guess what the encrypted text means? Ciphertext: Xli iriqc pyvow mr xli Plain text: The enemy lurks in the ………

Excellent! As you can see, in the Caesar cipher, one letter replaces another. That is why this is a monoalphabetic cipher. You can replace it by writing down the normal alphabet, and underneath a secret alphabet, starting on any letter.
